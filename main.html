Are you or a loved one struggling with Mental Health?

Our Mission

We chose to advocate for mental health awareness as well as initiative because collectively, we feel as though people of our society pay little attention to mental health and downplay it as well as its effects. We had never come across a platform to discuss mental health from the perspective of an individual that has had the struggle. Therefore, we aimed to be the change we want to see. How you might ask? By creating a platform that advocates and supports individuals that may struggle with various mental health conditions as well as loved ones who step in as caregivers. We aim to cover topics that aren’t generally discussed as well as provide resources to assist managing what may be a difficult time. Here, you are loved and supported.
